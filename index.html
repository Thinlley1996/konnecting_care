About Us

About Konnecting Care

Konnecting care is an organisation formed to offer services to people with disability under the National Disability Insurance Scheme. The vision of the organisation is to enable the disadvantaged in the community. Our main aim to achieve this by delivering the true essence of NDIS

Mission

To maximize independence, skills, ability, and improving quality of life.

Vision

Provide services of the highest quality that involves high professional and ethical standards. Our aim is to increase the independence, skills and quality of life for people who have support needs including people with a disability.
