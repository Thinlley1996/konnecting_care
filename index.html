About Us

About Konnecting Care

Konnecting care is an organisation formed to offer services to people with disability under the National Disability Insurance Scheme. The vision of the organisation is to enable the disadvantaged in the community. Our main aim to achieve this by delivering the true essence of NDIS
Mission

To maximize independence, skills, ability, and improving quality of life.

Vision

Provide services of the highest quality that involves high professional and ethical standards. Our aim is to increase the independence, skills and quality of life for people who have support needs including people with a disability.

PARTICIPATION IN COMMUNITY, SOCIAL AND CIVIC ACTIVITIES

By providing one-on-one support in the community, we aim to enable people to develop their autonomy/independence and achieve their full potential.

DAILY PERSONAL ACTIVITIES

Our goal is to increase people's autonomy, so they feel more comfortable doing daily chores like cleaning, laundry, shopping, and preparing meals on their own.

HIGH INTENSITY DAILY PERSONAL ACTIVITIES.

We strive to ensure people with high care needs have access to responsive, timely, competent, and appropriate supports to satisfy their needs.

DEVELOPMENT OF DAILY LIVING AND LIFE SKILLS

By focusing on participants' strengths, we will help them develop skills for daily living.

People and their caregivers can receive training and assistance to increase their ability to live a fulfilling life.

SPECIALIST POSITIVE BEHAVIOR SUPPORT

It is our pleasure to assist you if you have a Positive Behaviour Support Plan and need qualified professionals to implement it.

RESPITE CARE AND SHORT TERM ACCOMODATION

The program allows families and caregivers to spend time doing things they love while receiving premium services.

Innov Community Participation

As a community participation service provider, we support people with disabilities to develop skills that allow them to be fully included in community events, programs, and activities.

Group and Centre Based Activities

As part of our program, participants will be able to participate in group activities and centre-based activities designed to encourage social engagement and peer-to-peer learning.

Support Independent Living (SIL)

People with disabilities who are eligible for NDIS funding can opt to live independently through Supported Independent Living (SIL).

ASSISTANCE WITH TRAVEL AND TRANSPORT ARRANGEMENTS

As part of your participation in community life, economic activities, and daily life activities, we can provide you with transportation.

Top questions about Konnecting Care

Why Should I choose konnecting care ?

Konnecting care is an organisation formed to offer services to people with disability under the National Disability Insurance Scheme. The vision of the organisation is to enable the disadvantaged in the community. Our main aim to achieve this by delivering the true essence of NDIS as: The National Disability Insurance Scheme (NDIS) is the new way of providing support for Australians with a disability, their families and carers. As an insurance scheme, the NDIS takes a lifetime approach, investing in people with disability early to improve their outcomes later in life. Source: www.ndis.gov.au/about-us/what-ndis.html

Why was Konnecting care established?

• Provide a level of service that is beyond the expectations of the clients and their families
• Increase the independence, skills and quality of life for people who have support needs including people with a disability, and support the implementation of National Disability Insurance Scheme;
• Offer a service that maximises independence, ability, skills and improving quality of life of our clients
• Provide a nurturing and friendly work environment for the carers which encourages development
