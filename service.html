Konnecting care is put up on the principles of offering consistently high quality services with minimal disruption to the clients, and utmost focus on respect and dignity. We offer services to people with disabilities under the National Disability Insurance Scheme (NDIS). Our client choice service model with focus on enabling our clients to making independent decision making and connecting our clients with the wider community will make us special from our competition. Our main goal is to provide services of utmost respect and dignity to our customers by empowering them to integrate into the wider community and create a work culture where employees at all levels have a “can-do” attitude and put in their best efforts to achieve outcomes that meet clients’ goals.
